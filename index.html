Research Interests

Stochastic processes, applied probability, mechanism design with applications to:

  • Learning and Control in non-stationary environments

  • Operations of online platforms

  • Optimization of spatial queueing/matching and transportation networks

  • Scheduling theory

  • Cloud computing As part of research, I am working with third party logistics, and farm real estate platforms. I am also interested in applying tools from Operations Research, Econometrics, and AI/ML to improve access and lower barriers to mental healthcare.
