Varun Gupta – Bio

  • 2016 – Present: Associate Professor, University of Chicago, Booth School of Business.

  • 2012 – 2016: Assistant Professor, University of Chicago, Booth School of Business.

  • 2011 – 2012: Postdoctoral Researcher, Google Research (NY).

  • 2004 – 2011: Ph.D. student, Computer Science Department, Carnegie Mellon University. (Advisor: Mor Harchol-Balter)
    Thesis title: Stochastic Models and Analysis for Resource Management in Server Farms [thesis]

  • Summer 2008: Research Intern, Alcatel-Lucent Bell Labs.

  • Summer 2006: Research Intern, Microsoft Research (Cambridge, UK).

  • 2000 – 2004: B.Tech. student, Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Techology (Delhi, India). President's Gold Medal winner
    Thesis title: Algorithms for Computational Biology: Sequence Analysis
